The


                                                                                                                                                                                                             fog


                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      comes





on little

                                                                                                                                                      cat feet.







                                                                                                                                              it sits
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 looking
over
       harbor
                and
                        city





                                                                                                                       on silent haunches









and                                                                                                                then                                                                                                                             moves                                                                                                                     on.
