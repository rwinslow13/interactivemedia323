The fog
   comes



                                                                                                                                                                                                                       on little
                                                                                                                                                                                                                       cat feet.







                                                                                                                        It sits looking over
                                                                                                                                                      harbor and city







                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               on silent haunches




and then









































                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      moves on.
